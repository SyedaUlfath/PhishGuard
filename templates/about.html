About Phishing URL Detector

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information like passwords, credit card details, or personal data. They often use deceptive URLs that look similar to trusted websites.

How Our Detection Works
Heuristic Analysis

Analyzes URL structure, suspicious keywords, and domain characteristics to identify potential threats.

Machine Learning

Uses trained models to classify URLs based on patterns learned from known phishing and legitimate sites.

Brand Impersonation

Detects attempts to impersonate major brands like Apple, Google, Facebook, and financial institutions.

Pattern Recognition

Identifies suspicious URL patterns, unusual domains, and other indicators of malicious intent.

Disclaimer: This tool provides analysis based on heuristics and machine learning. It should be used as a supplementary security measure. Always exercise caution when visiting unfamiliar websites and verify the legitimacy of suspicious URLs through multiple sources.
Stay Safe Online
  • Always verify URLs before clicking, especially in emails or messages
  • Look for HTTPS encryption and valid SSL certificates
  • Be suspicious of URLs with unusual domains or excessive special characters
  • Never enter personal information on suspicious websites
  • Use two-factor authentication when available
